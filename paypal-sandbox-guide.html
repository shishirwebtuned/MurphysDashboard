🧪 PayPal Sandbox Test Account Setup

Create fake test accounts to test payments without using real money

❌ PROBLEM: You're trying to use your REAL Nepali PayPal account in SANDBOX (testing mode)
✅ SOLUTION: Create FAKE test accounts from PayPal Developer Dashboard

1️⃣ Go to PayPal Developer Dashboard

Open PayPal Developer Dashboard →

Login with your real PayPal account (yes, use your Nepali account)

2️⃣ Navigate to Sandbox Accounts

  1. In the left sidebar, click Testing Tools
  2. Click Sandbox Accounts
  3. Click the Create Account button

3️⃣ Create a Personal (Buyer) Test Account

Field What to Enter
Account Type Personal (this is the buyer/customer)
Country United States (required for USD)
Email Auto-generated (like sb-xxxxx@personal.example.com)
Password Set your own or use auto-generated
Balance $500.00 (give it test money)

Click Create button

4️⃣ Get Your Test Account Credentials

  1. Find your new Personal account in the list
  2. Click the ⋮ (three dots) menu
  3. Click View/Edit Account
  4. Copy the Email and Password
TEST ACCOUNT EXAMPLE:
Email: sb-12abc3@personal.example.com
Password: YourTestPassword123
Balance: $500.00 USD

5️⃣ Test Payment on Your Website

  1. Go back to your billing page
  2. Click "Pay with PayPal" button
  3. When PayPal login appears:
🛑 DON'T USE YOUR REAL ACCOUNT!
Use the test account email and password you just created
  1. Login with: sb-xxxxx@personal.example.com
  2. Complete the payment
  3. 🎉 Success! Payment will go through
